Who we are

Born from
the flame.

Little Phoenix PR is an entertainment-focused public relations agency built for artists, performers, and the people who move culture forward. We work in music, live events, film, and the spaces where those worlds collide.

We believe every act of genuine artistry deserves to be heard, not buried. Our work is personal, relentless, and built on real relationships with the press, the public, and the industry.
